Dealer questions, answered.

What builders, lumber yards, and marine suppliers ask before signing on.

Is my territory protected?

Yes. We sign a limited number of authorized dealers per market so you are not competing with the dealer down the street on the same product. Apply early to lock in your area before someone else does. We will confirm exact territory details when we talk.

Is there a minimum order to become a dealer?

We keep the entry simple and reasonable so you can stock the system and prove it out without an oversized commitment. We will walk you through opening order and restock terms based on your market size when you apply.

What lead times can I quote my customers?

AmeriDex is manufactured in the USA by Patwin Plastics, so supply is domestic and predictable. We give every dealer current lead times up front so you can quote install dates with confidence.

What does AmeriDex do to help me sell?

You get real board and seal samples, sales sheets, counter and showroom displays, social creative, and homeowner-facing advertising that drives demand into your market. We treat your sell-through as our job too.

Who installs it, and is training available?

AmeriDex installs on a conventional 16-inch on-center frame, no special substructure. We provide installation guidance and instructional videos so your builders are confident on their first job.

What's the warranty I'm selling behind?

A 25-year residential limited warranty and a 10-year limited commercial warranty on the deck board system. A long written warranty closes hesitant buyers at your counter.
