How It Works

Three steps. One dry deck.

01

Frame your deck like normal.

Standard joist layout. Standard framing. No special substructure. AmeriDex installs on a conventional frame at 16" on center.

02

Install AmeriDex boards with the integrated seal.

Each board carries the water-diverting seal as part of its profile. Tongue-and-groove engagement creates a continuous waterproof deck surface.

03

Water sheds away. Under-deck space stays bone dry.

Rain runs across the deck, gets carried out by the seal, and falls clear of the joists. The space below stays dry, finished, and usable.
