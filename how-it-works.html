How It Works

How the Dryspace System Works.

AmeriDex isn't a tray hung under your deck. It IS the deck. The water-diverting seal is engineered into every board, so the structure underneath stays dry by design.

Anatomy

One board. Four working layers.

Every AmeriDex board carries the full system. Here's how the layers stack up from the surface down.

1 AmeriDex Wood-Alternative Board
2 Integrated Water-Diverting Seal
3 Hidden Starborn Fastener System
4 Standard Framing Stays Dry
  1. 1

    Wood-alternative board face

    A premium wood-alternative cap with authentic grain. Won't splinter, rot, or warp. Comes in 7 finishes.

  2. 2

    Integrated tongue-and-groove seal

    Each board carries a water-diverting seal as part of its profile. Boards lock together to form a continuous waterproof surface.

  3. 3

    Hidden Starborn fastener system

    Starborn epoxy or stainless screws and plugs. No visible fastener heads. Premium hardware included.

  4. 4

    Framing below stays dry

    Water sheds across the deck and exits past the joists. Your framing, electrical, and finishes underneath stay bone dry.

By Design

Why new construction only.

This is a feature, not a limitation.

AmeriDex is engineered into your build from the joists up. That's exactly why it works. Retrofit drainage systems hang underneath your deck and fail. AmeriDex IS the deck. The seal is part of every board, not a tray bolted on after the fact. That's why we can warranty it for 25 years.
